burger countdown

We personally tried several of Nashville's most recommended burgers and ranked them according to a well thought out scoring system.

Burgers were ranked on the following criteria:

  • Taste (0-10): The overall taste and finish of the burger.

  • Quality (0-5): The quality, freshness of the ingredients, and the craftsmanship of the burger.

  • Temperature (0-3): The temperature of the meat, how well the natural juices and flavor of the meat is announced.

  • Presentation (0-2): Not just how well the burger looks plated, but how original the sandwich is and how easy it is to handle and savor throughout the meal.
20 is the highest possible total and they are scored in .5 point increments
